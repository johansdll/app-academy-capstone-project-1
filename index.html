Favorite Genres

Isekai

A subgenre of fantasy in which a character is suddenly transported from their world into a new or unfamiliar one via either reincarnation, summoning, or a god transmigrating them. The setting is usually but not always, medieval fantasy with swords and magic.

Shounen

The stories can be serious, humorous or both. Some of the themes that shonen manga cover are action, adventure, sports, science fiction, horror, mystery, and mythology.

Romantic Subtext

A narrative which can at first appear to be Romance. While the story will set the couple up for heartwarming or embarrassing moments, these encounters will not lead to any significant romantic development.
